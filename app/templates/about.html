About This Project

This tracker focuses on transparent beneficiary analysis: not whether a bill is good or bad, but who is most likely to benefit and why.

Methodology

  • Layer A applies deterministic rules to policy area, subjects, and committees.
  • Layer B uses lobbying filing matches and AI-assisted suggestions.
  • AI suggestions remain pending until owner approval in admin review.
  • Every public beneficiary claim must have inspectable evidence.

Data Sources

  • Congress.gov API
  • GovInfo Search Service
  • Senate LDA API
  • USAFacts (optional context imports)
